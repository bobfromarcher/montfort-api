Title: Montfort — Agent Infrastructure for the AI Era

Description:
Montfort is the operating layer between your LLMs and your business logic. One voice command dispatches work to any agent, chains multiple agents into pipelines, or broadcasts to your entire fleet. Every interaction is tracked, timed, and billable.

What you get:
- Voice dispatch: Push-to-talk agent commands using local Whisper (works air-gapped)
- 8 pre-built agent templates: Code, Security, Analytics, Support, Content, PM, Research, DevOps — 1-click deploy
- Scheduled dispatches: Run agents on autopilot (daily standups, weekly audits, etc.)
- Agent chains: Route through multiple agents in sequence (architect → coder → reviewer)
- Discord integration: Agents as team members in your channels
- Billing infrastructure: Stripe-connected, per-dispatch metering, plan enforcement
- Audit + analytics: Every dispatch logged, latency tracked, health monitored
- On-prem capable: Run behind your firewall. Zero data leaves your network.

For developers: Free tier with 3 agents and 1,000 dispatches/month. No credit card.
For teams: $49/mo Professional — unlimited agents, 50K dispatches, full analytics.
For enterprise: Custom pricing. Air-gapped deploy. SOC 2 / HIPAA / ITAR ready.

We built this because the AI era needs infrastructure — not just models. Montfort is the middleware that turns AI agents into a manageable, billable, auditable operation.
