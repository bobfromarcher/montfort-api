DATA PROCESSING AGREEMENT (DPA) DE MONTFORT LLC Last Updated: April 12, 2026 This DPA supplements the Terms of Service between De Montfort LLC ("Processor") and the customer ("Controller"). 1. SCOPE This DPA applies to the processing of personal data by Montfort on behalf of the Controller in connection with the Services. 2. ROLES Controller: The customer who determines the purposes and means of processing personal data. Processor: De Montfort LLC, processing personal data on behalf of the Controller. 3. DATA CATEGORIES - End user prompts and commands submitted to agents - Agent responses and generated content - Voice recordings (transient, deleted after transcription) - Usage metadata (timestamps, latency, status codes) - Authentication credentials (encrypted) 4. PROCESSING PURPOSES Processing is limited to: delivering Services, maintaining system health, preventing abuse, and fulfilling contractual obligations. 5. PROCESSOR OBLIGATIONS - Process data only on documented instructions of the Controller - Ensure persons authorized to process data are under confidentiality obligations - Implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures (AES-256, TLS 1.3, access controls) - Engage sub-processors only with Controller authorization - Assist Controller in responding to data subject requests - Notify Controller of personal data breaches within 72 hours - Provide information necessary for compliance audits 6. SUB-PROCESSORS Current sub-processors: - Stripe, Inc. (payment processing) - United States - Cloud infrastructure provider (hosting) - United States Controller will be notified of changes to sub-processors with 30 days' notice. 7. DATA TRANSFERS For international transfers outside the EEA, Montfort relies on Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission. 8. SECURITY MEASURES - Encryption at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3) - Role-based access control with principle of least privilege - Regular security assessments and penetration testing - Incident response plan with 72-hour breach notification - Employee security training and background checks 9. DATA RETENTION Data is retained per the Privacy Policy. Upon termination, data is deleted within 30 days unless Controller requests export. 10. AUDIT RIGHTS Controller may audit Processor's compliance with this DPA once per year, with 30 days' notice. Processor will cooperate and provide access to relevant systems and records. 11. ON-PREMISES DEPLOYMENT For on-prem deployments, Montfort acts as a software provider only. No data is processed by Montfort. This DPA applies only to cloud-hosted Services. 12. TERM AND TERMINATION This DPA remains in effect for the duration of the Services agreement. Upon termination, Processor will delete or return all personal data within 30 days.
