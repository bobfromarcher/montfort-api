PRIVACY POLICY DE MONTFORT LLC Last Updated: April 12, 2026 1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT - Account Information: Email, company name, billing information - Usage Data: Agent dispatches, prompts, responses, latency metrics, error rates - Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers - Voice Data: Audio recordings for transcription (processed locally via Whisper in on-prem deployments; processed server-side only in cloud deployments, deleted after transcription) 2. HOW WE USE INFORMATION - To provide and maintain the Services - To process billing and manage subscriptions - To monitor system health and prevent abuse - To improve Services through aggregate analytics - To comply with legal obligations 3. DATA SHARING We do NOT sell your data. We share data only with: - Service providers (Stripe for billing, cloud infrastructure providers) - Law enforcement when required by law - Business partners with your explicit consent 4. DATA RETENTION - Account data: Retained while account is active + 30 days post-termination - Dispatch logs: 90 days (Enterprise: configurable) - Voice recordings: Deleted immediately after transcription (cloud) or never leave your infrastructure (on-prem) - Billing records: 7 years (legal requirement) 5. DATA SECURITY - AES-256 encryption for credentials at rest - TLS 1.3 for data in transit - Context isolation and sandboxing in desktop applications - Rate limiting and input sanitization on all endpoints - SOC 2 Type II compliance roadmap (target: Q4 2026) 6. YOUR RIGHTS - Access: Request a copy of your data - Correction: Update inaccurate data - Deletion: Request deletion of your data - Portability: Export your data in machine-readable format - Objection: Object to processing of your data 7. ON-PREMISES DEPLOYMENTS For on-prem/air-gapped deployments, ALL data processing occurs within your infrastructure. Montfort has no access to your data. Support access requires your explicit authorization per incident. 8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY Services are not intended for anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children. 9. INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS Data is processed in the United States. For EU users, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for lawful transfer. 10. CHANGES We may update this Policy with 30 days' notice. Continued use constitutes acceptance. 11. CONTACT Data Protection Officer: privacy@montfort.com De Montfort LLC Florida, United States
