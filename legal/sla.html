SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA) DE MONTFORT LLC Last Updated: April 12, 2026 This SLA applies to Montfort Enterprise plan subscribers only. 1. SERVICE COMMITMENT Montfort targets 99.9% uptime for the API, dashboard, and agent dispatch services measured monthly. 2. MEASUREMENT Uptime is measured as: (Total Minutes in Month - Downtime Minutes) / Total Minutes in Month × 100 Excluded from downtime calculation: - Scheduled maintenance (communicated 72 hours in advance, max 4 hours/month) - Force majeure events - Issues caused by customer configuration or third-party LLM provider outages - Issues with customer's network or infrastructure 3. SERVICE CREDITS If uptime falls below the commitment: | Uptime Percentage | Service Credit | |-------------------|---------------| | 99.0% - 99.9% | 10% of monthly fee | | 95.0% - 99.0% | 25% of monthly fee | | Below 95.0% | 50% of monthly fee | Service credits are applied to the next billing cycle. Maximum credit is 50% of monthly fee. 4. CLAIMS To claim a service credit, customer must submit a claim within 30 days of the incident to support@montfort.com with: - Affected service and timeframe - Description of impact - Supporting evidence (logs, monitoring data) 5. SUPPORT TIERS - Critical (service unavailable): 1-hour response, 4-hour resolution target - High (major feature impaired): 4-hour response, 8-hour resolution target - Medium (minor feature impaired): 8-hour response, 24-hour resolution target - Low (cosmetic/informational): 24-hour response, 72-hour resolution target 6. ON-PREMISES For on-prem deployments, Montfort provides software updates and remote support. SLA commitments apply to support response times only, not infrastructure uptime (which is customer-managed). 7. MAINTENANCE WINDOWS Scheduled maintenance occurs on Sundays 02:00-06:00 UTC. Emergency maintenance may occur with 4 hours' notice. 8. EXCLUSIONS This SLA does not cover: - Latency of third-party LLM providers - Customer-introduced bugs or misconfigurations - Data loss due to customer failure to back up - Degradation caused by exceeding rate limits or dispatch quotas 9. MODIFICATIONS Montfort may modify this SLA with 30 days' written notice.
